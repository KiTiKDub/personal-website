Kalimba Sample Pack
Create your own beautiful melodies with the help of your own Kalimba, straight into your DAW!

I sampled my own Kalimba, and created everything that you need to make your own track.

Inside this pack you will get:

  • 13 chords
  • 10 Melodies (Dry & Wet)
  • 23 One Shots
  • 17 Notes (Build your own chords!)
  • Project file seen in video! (Ableton file)
