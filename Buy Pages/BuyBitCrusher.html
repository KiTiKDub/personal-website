Simple BitCrusher

Easy Bit Crusher to add to the 'Simple' Suite.

  • Reduce Bit depth to add some crunch your sound
  • Increase the rate to get that Lofi feel.
  • Mix in and out how much of the bit crushed signal you want
  • Adjust the cutoff of a low pass filter to the crushed signal
