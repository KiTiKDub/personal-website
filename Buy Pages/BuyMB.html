MULTIBAND COMPRESSOR - FREE

Take your production skills to the next level with this FREE Multiband Compressor!

This Multiband Compressor brings you complete control with any sound you through at.

With this compressor you can:
  • Dial in any part of the frequency spectrum with adjustable crossover dials
  • Adjust the attack, release, threshold, and ratio for all three bands.
  • Bypass, solo, and mute all bands to hear every detail of the sound
  • Use the spectrum analyzer to see exactly how your sound looks and easily adjust as needed
  • Dial in the exact threshold you need with threshold graph mapped straight onto the spectrum analyzer
  • See exactly how the compressor is responding to your changes with threshold graph
