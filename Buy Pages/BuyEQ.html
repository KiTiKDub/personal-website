3 BAND EQ - FREE

Need a simple EQ to spice up your production? Look no further!

Here is a simple EQ that gets quick fixes on your sounds, or on your bus.
  • You get everything you expect with your high pass and low pass filters, and mid band filter to boost or lower any frequencies you desire
  • Adjust your slopes of the high & low pass filters, ranging from 6 db/oct to 24!
  • Control is in your hands. Toggle on off each band, and see where your cut offs are with ease with the EQ curve
All code is open source and can be found in the github link found in the read me of this download.
