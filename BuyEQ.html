Three-band EQ (FREE)
Need a simple EQ to spice up your production? Look no further!

Here is a simple EQ that get quick fixes on your sounds, or on your bus.
  • You get everything you expect with your high pass and low pass filters, and mid band filter to boost or lower any frequenices you desire
  • Adjust your slopes of the high & low pass filters, ranging from 6 db/oct to 24!
  • Control is in your hands. Toggle on off each band, and see where your cut offs are with ease with the EQ curve
This EQ was created from a tutorial from programmingformusicans.com. Check out there website if you are interested in developing your own plugins!

All code is open source and can be found in the github link found in the read me of this download.
