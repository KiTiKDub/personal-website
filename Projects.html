Partical Explosion (C++)

Visual rendering useing SDL2 to map particles onto the screen. The particles start at the center of the screen and spiral outword. The particles change color, with incoperated motion blur.

Source Code can be found here: https://github.com/KiTiKDub/VisualParticalExplosion

Simple EQ (C++)

3 Band EQ that cut lows, highs, and a peak filter to boost or cut mids. Change filter slope on 4 different settings for lows and highs. Turn off and on any band. See the filter band in real time at the top of the window.

Source Code can be found here: https://github.com/KiTiKDub/SimpleEQ

3-band Compressor (C++)

Multiband compressor with bands for low, mids, and highs. Each band has adjustable crossover frequncies to dial in exactly where you need your compression.

Each band has adjustable attack, release, threshold, and ratio, with the ability to mute, solo, and bypass the band.

Visualize your compression with a spectrum analyzer to see where you may need to dial down your sound. Each band has the threshold mapped to the analyzer so you can see exactly when your compression will kick in. The analyzer will also show the response of the compression so you can see exactly how it is responding to your input.

This is a great free compressor if your DAW does not have built in multiband, download today!


Source code can be found here https://github.com/KiTiKDub/MBComp
